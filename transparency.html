​​Guide Star India Transparency Key Award

Hunger Free India Foundation NGO is one of the recipient of Guide Star India Transparency Key award for 2020. GuideStar India is India’s largest and most reliable NGO information repository.


We participated in their certification programme to  undergo different levels of due diligence and been awarded the Guide Star India Transparency Key award for the past 3 consecutive years.


Guide Star India validated all our documents, checks our compliance standards before giving this award.

Awards & Recognition

We believe that Awards are external validation of our work and a proof that we are doing things right. Below are some of the key awards we received for the volunteering work we do. 

Leader in Volunteering Engagement 

This award from iVolunteer is one of the most prestigious awards any non-profit can receive in India. Applications are received across India, people vote to select the top-10 NGO’s. Finally, a group of experts selects the winner.

Radio City Chennai Citizen award

Radio City FM selected Hunger Free India Foundation founder Sarath Babu as one of the 10 members for their Chennai Citizen award in 2020. This award is for taking initiatives to provide scholarships for the underprivileged.

Watch the 3 mins award video

Best person of the year award

One of the leading magazine and TV channel in Tamilnadu - Puthiya Thalaimurai selected Hunger Free India Foundation founder Karthee Vidya for their ‘Best person of the year-2010’ award. Only 2 person have been selected from entire Tamilnadu for this award.

Commitment for Scholarship

If you donate for our ‘I am the Change’ Scholarship program, we tag a student to you in Oct/Nov whom you can meet or call.


You can even be part of our student selection process if you are in Chennai, Coimbatore or Madurai.


If you are in other locations, you can help by volunteering to train our students on Spoken English through phone as part of our Speak Out initiative.
