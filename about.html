About Us

HUNGER -FREE INDIA is a registered, non-governmental, non-religious and non-profit social service organization, established in 2010, with the primary objective of serving quality food to the needy people and there by making the vision of HUNGER-FREE INDIA. SarathBabu Elumalai,BITS PILANI & IIMA ALUMNI who in his childhood has witnessed his struggling mother who was sacrificing her own food to feed her growing children. This struggle is now turned into a vision to make HUNGER-FREE INDIA.

About the Founder

I was born and brought up in a slum in Madipakkam in Chennai. I have two elder sisters and two younger brothers and my mother was the sole breadwinner of the family. It was really tough for my mother to bring up five kids on her meager salary. She sold Idlis in the mornings, worked for the mid-day meal at the school during daytime and taught at the adult education programme of the Indian government, thus doing three different jobs to bring us up and educate us. “My mother, according to me, is the most successful entrepreneur

Our Vision

To provide quality food for the needy people & to provide the basic platform to achieve HUNGER-FREE INDIA
