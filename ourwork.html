What we do in Everest?

Hunger Free India Foundation is one of the biggest youth run NGOs in India. We have 15,000+ volunteers with our focus on motivating individuals to volunteer at least once a month (12/365) and make a difference by providing quality education to financially challenged children through volunteering.

First Penguin Club

First Penguin Club (FPC) is a 100 hour Skill development training program for our scholars.

From Public speaking to Problem sloving, we teach all the essential employability skills to our students through FPC initiative.

Walk to Cycle

Many rural students walk more than 3 km a day to attend school. We want to change this situation of the children by gifting them a cycle.

We have gifted bicycles to more than 750 students so far and help them reach home soon.
