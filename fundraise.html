Many children drop out after school and don't go to college due to financial challenges. This number is especially high for Parentless and Single parented students.

Team Everest identifies such children and sponsor them for their college education. 

We are currently sponsoring the graduation of 400 parentless & single parented students in Chennai as part of our ‘I am the Change’ scholarship program. 

Our Volunteers becomes fundraisers, reach out to their friends & family and raise funds to help the kids join college.

Fundraisers create 10 times more impact than donors.

You too can be a changemaker and change their future forever by becoming a fundraiser. It’s an online fundraising and our team can guide you on what you need to do.
