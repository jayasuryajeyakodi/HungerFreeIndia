Promoting Volunteering culture in your Organization

From 30 mins in the desk to 3 months, we have designed various programs for everyone to volunteer. We help build your employee volunteering program, orient employees and come up with a year long volunteering calendar for your organization. 


Find the various Employee volunteering programs which can be a part of your CSR initiatives.

Gift Painting Workshop

Team Everest conducts various events for School Children. We need prizes for these events. Such prizes are made, designed by volunteers during 'Gift Making Workshops'. 

'B' for Blackboard

Many Schools lack proper blackboards. We take volunteers to paint these blackboards to enable schools to have good blackboards.

Talent Search

It gives a platform for government school kids to participate in various extracurricular contests and showcase their skills. The program is designed to give equal opportunities to all kids.

Interview Simulation

A full day workshop on interview skills by employees for College students. Employees take interviews and guide students on the interview process to overcome the fear of interviews.
